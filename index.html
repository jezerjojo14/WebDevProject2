Welcome to my website!

Hello and welcome to Minimalist Origami Designs.
I know some may call my work "lazy" or "uninspired", but the truth is that only a real artist like myself could even come close to understanding my work.

"But sir! Surely anyone could could have done what you're doing!" you say. Well, has anyone done what I'm doing? What I'm doing here is art. I do not see this as a website for showcasing origami. I see it as more of an "experience".

Here I will keep an archive of all my origami designs (Showcase Page) and I will occasionally jot down various insights that may come to me on this journey (Journal Page).
